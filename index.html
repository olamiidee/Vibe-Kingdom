What does vibe mean?

Vibe: (noun) a distinctive feeling or quality capable of being sensed

Vibe: (verb) to be in harmony; to convey a sense of

This is how Merriam Webster defines the word "Vibe." While these are just words on your screen, let me paint you a picture. Vibes can portray the dark, drizzly afternoons where the rain pitter patters across your roof, or it can express the warm, fuzzy feeling shared between two two friends chuckling in laughter. The word "vibe" is a special, everchanging but intangible thing. That's why we understand your slight confusion when we say, ""No purge. No grinding.."

"...Just Vibe."

Because what does it mean to "Just Vibe[?]" We wish to find genuine fans of our artist's hard work and show love by Knighting (WL) them. We welcome you to share our work and our project across your platforms if you truly support it. Or even draw inspiration from our illustrations to create a piece of fan art that pays homage to our artwork. (It doesn't need to be perfect, just the thought that counts!) You might just see your name turn Orange one day. On the other hand, we know people are also here for the flip, which we totally understand, and while we welcome you all with open arms, we encourage you to find a community within. Even the seed of shared love for the art can be grown into a big, strong tree. Remember, a kingdom is an entity made up of countless unique individuals coming together from many walks of life, just like you.

Now, maybe this makes a bit more sense to you, or maybe some of you are still scratching your heads. That's okay, that's the point! While you can define "Vibe" on a piece of paper, it simultaneously abstractly lives within you. Find your vibe, share good vibes, and see where it brings you. The team will continuously hand-pick all Knight (WL) spots to reward those that we truly believe deserve it.

Now go forth into the Kingdom and beyond to share good vibes.
